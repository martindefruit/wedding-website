Getting There

We chose Pago del Vicario for our wedding because of our family connection and love for wine. If you tried Manuscrito, it comes from here!

Madrid → Bodega Pago del Vicario (~200 km)

The closest airport to Pago del Vicario is Madrid Barajas Airport (MAD). From Madrid, the vineyard is located approximately 200km south in Ciudad Real province, a 2-hour drive or a 1-hour train ride from Madrid.

Car Rental

Rent a car at Madrid Airport for the most flexibility. The drive takes approximately 2 hours via the A-4 highway.

Recommended

Train

Take the high-speed train (AVE) from Madrid Atocha station to Ciudad Real. The journey takes about 1 hour.

Book on Trainline

Taxi / Private Transfer

Book a private taxi or transfer service from Madrid Airport directly to the venue or your accommodation.
